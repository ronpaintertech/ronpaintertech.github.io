About Me

I have extensive expertise in a large variety of web and multimedia technologies. This variety has made it possible for me to build upon my experiences to explore new and exciting technologies. I began as a desktop application developer which led to opportunities for intranet, then internet, web application development. My eLearning course development grew by adding multimedia skills to my web development experience. While in talent and development at BNY Mellon, I developed a strategy to incorporate mobile learning and delivery into eLearning and multimedia communications, which again enabled me to expand my technology universe. By utilizing my Java, web and multimedia developer skills, I am taking new steps into mobile development by learning Android app development using Kotlin.

I am currently enrolled in a few Udemy courses as steps to learning Android development and I am at various stages of completion. My current courses are Android Java Masterclass - Become an App Developer and Kotlin for Java Developers. To bring myself up to date on the latest version of Java, I also am reviewing Java Programming Masterclass for Software Developers. My plans after those courses are the Google Udacity courses on Kotlin and Android.
